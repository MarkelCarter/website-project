Why we work out!!

Exercising helps people lose weight and lowers the risk of some diseases. When you are exercising you are losing fat around your body. People who are working out everyday are the people you see with the most fit bodies (everybody wants a body like those people). In order to get that body you have to workout!!!

Benefits of Working Out

  • It helps keep your thinking, learning, and judgment skills sharp as you age.
  • It can help you stop smoking.
  • Not only does it improve your physical looks, it also improves your physical health and moods.
  • People who exercise are happier.
  • People who work out sleep better.
  • Working out makes us smarter.
